Biryani


Ingredients

  • chicken 1/2 kg
  • Onions 3 or 4
  • Tomato 4
  • Potatoes(opional) 2
  • Oil 1/2 cup
  • Ginger & Garlic Paste, 1 tbsp
  • Red chili powder 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric 1/2 tsp
  • Corainder 1 bunch
  • Fresh mint 1/2 bunch
  • Yogurt 1/2 cup

    • Method

    1. Instructions:

      2. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, mix the chicken pieces with Yogurt, Ginger, Garlic, Paste, chili powder, Turmeric powder,cumin powder,and salt.Let it marinate for at leas,t 1 hour preferably longer for better flavour

    2. Cook the rice:

      3. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the soaked and drained rice along with wholes spices (cardamom, cloves,cinnamon,)and salt.Cook until the rice is 70-80% done.Drain the rice and set aside.

    3. Fry the Onions

      4. In a separate pan, heat ghee and oil. Fry the thinly sliced onions until golden brown and crispy. Remove from the oil and set aside.

    4. Layering the Biryani:

      5. In a heavy-bottomed pot,sptead a layer of marinated chicken at the bottom. Top it with a layer of cooked rice. Sprinkle some chopped tomatoes,green chilies,coriander leaves, mint leaves, and fried onions Repeat the layers until all the chicken and rice are used up. Finished with a layer of rice on top.

    5. Cooking the biryani:

      6. Cover the pot tightly with a lid or aluminum foil. Cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the flavours have melded

      together You can alsa place a tawa (griddle) underneath the pot to prevent burning
